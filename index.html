Snack's Twitch Integration for Darkest Dungeon

General Commands

  • !bal - check balance
  • !join - Your hero will join the queue for the adventure
  • !heist - triggers heist minigame to gamble with your points, high risk high reward
  • !gamble - triggers dice rolling minigame to game with your points, low risk low reward
  • !bet - Wagers an amount to determine the outcome of the next dungeon crawl

List of upgrades for Hero

  • !buy weapon - 75 - Requests upgrade for your current weapon
  • !buy armor - 60 - Requests upgrade for your current weapon
  • !buy Hero - 10 - Requests to be assigned to a hero
  • !buy skill - 40 - Requests upgrade for a skill for your hero
  • !buy affliction - 10 - Requests removal of stress/affliction from your hero
  • !buy quirk - 35 - Requests removal of a quirk from your hero
  • !buy disease - 25 - Requests removal of a disease from your hero
  • !buy trinket - 20 - Requests a trinket for your hero
